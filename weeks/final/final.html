this is a flowchart of the actions i wanted the three servo motors to do.

  1. press button.
  2. turn motor 1 in direction x for t seconds. turn motor 1 in direction y for t seconds.
  3. turn motor 2 in direction y for t seconds. turn motor 1 in direction x for t seconds.
  4. turn motor 3 in direction z for t seconds. turn motor 1 in direction z' for t seconds.
  5. check for next button press.
